THIS WEBSITE IS NOT PART OF OUR SUBMISSION! IT IS JUST FOR USERS TO DOWNLOAD THE APP, AND SHOULD NOT BE JUDGED! GO TO OUR CODE URL TO SEE OUR WORK, OR FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS TO DOWNLOAD OUR APP BELOW.

Time Machine allows the user to become a time traveler from the comfort of their modern smartphone. We do all the hard work, grabbing data from reliable government APIs in order to historically display the history of the area in a beautiful format that is easy to understand and enjoy. Time Machine was created in one weekend at the Sydney GovHack hackathon.

- Crowdsourced!
- Reliable and Accurate!
- Beautiful!

If you're using an Android device, simply open up Google Play and search for Time Travelers AU.

Datasets used in our project.

  • State Records (private and public, some supplied at GovHack) - 12GB of files and metadata.
  • Trove - Images, events, newspaper articles and metadata.
  • ABS.Stat - Crime data.
  • State Library of New South Wales (via data.gov.au) - Photographs of Sydney.
